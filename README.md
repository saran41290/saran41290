### 🚀 Featured Projects

**[Hiring Intelligence ETL Automation Platform](https://github.com/saran41290) · Jul 2026**
`Python` `FastAPI` `Pandas` `SQLite` `BeautifulSoup` `n8n` `Power BI` `REST API` `Git`
- Production-style ETL pipeline extracting, validating, cleaning, transforming, and enriching 4,300+ job postings from 26 companies into analytics-ready datasets.
- Automated the full pipeline with FastAPI and n8n — API-driven orchestration, centralized logging, error handling, and execution monitoring.
- Interactive Power BI dashboard covering hiring trends, salary analysis, skill demand, geographic insights, and work-mode distribution.

**[Customer Support Analytics Pipeline](https://github.com/saran41290) · Jun 2026**
`Python` `n8n` `Google Forms` `Google Sheets` `Gmail API` `Looker Studio` `Pandas`
- End-to-end customer support analytics pipeline automating ticket collection, validation, cleaning, transformation, and reporting.
- Automated ticket ingestion, email notifications, workflow orchestration, and data sync across Forms, Sheets, Gmail, and Looker Studio — replacing manual processing with near real-time monitoring.
- Interactive Looker Studio dashboard tracking ticket volume trends, sentiment, SLA compliance, category distribution, and response-time KPIs.

**[Erode Mandi Price Volatility & Market Opportunity Analysis](https://github.com/saran41290) · Apr 2026**
`Power BI` `Power Query` `DAX` `Excel`
- Analyzed 2021–2025 mandi price data across 104 commodities and 26 markets to surface volatility, seasonal movement, and market opportunity patterns.
- Star-schema Power BI model (1 fact table, 3 dimension tables, 20 DAX measures) supporting KPI tracking and time-based comparisons.
- 3 dashboard pages pinpointing volatile commodities, high-risk markets, and key opportunity indicators.

**[Hospital Management Analytical Dashboard — Capstone Project](https://github.com/saran41290) · Mar 2026**
`SQL` `Excel` `Power BI`
- Analyzed hospital operations data (2023–2025) spanning 8,000 patients, 200 doctors, 25,000 appointments, 18,000 treatments, and 25,000 billing records.
- Multi-table Power BI dashboard tracking revenue, patient behavior, doctor-level performance, and operational trends.
- Surfaced ₹63.21M in tracked revenue, an 81.7% returning-patient rate, and Orthopaedics as the top-earning department at ₹11.8M.
